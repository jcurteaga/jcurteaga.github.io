What is Lau Board?

Originally, Lau Board is a tribute to Mr.Lau and his amazing quotes through a soundboard app running on iPhones, written by Ben Cullen. Unfortunately, the App Store review team decided to reject this app because it is not "useful" enough, i.e. only Mr. Lau's students will find this interesting. But we all know, these inspiring quotes are for everyone.
